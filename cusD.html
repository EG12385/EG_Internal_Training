Reviewing a Customer's Current Discount Plan



Customer on a Voice Package

NOTE: The structure of a voice package, consists of a pre-defined amount of 100% free minutes, and once used up there is a specified rate charged per minute. This is a postpaid billing structure, and the customer might be billed for extra voice usage at the end of a billing cycle. Each voice discount package is available only for the current billing cycle, and no minutes left is carried over to the next billing cycle.

Customer on a Voice Bundle

There is currently two voice bundle structures, as follows:

Prepaid Discount Bundle - A

  • Credit Limit is set to 0.00, available funds is added as a 'reserve' and to allow voice calls.
  • Minute based discount plan is build and applied to the customer profile to provide the agreed amount of 100% free minutes.
  • Billing is done monthly, as per the amount specified on the SOF, payable for the provided voice bundle.
  • Should the minutes be depleted, the customer needs to make a payment to have available funds in order to allow outgoing calls.
  • For this discount plan, default voice tariffs may be applied, or a specified rate can be applied.
  • Discount minutes not used, will not be carried over to the next month. The discount is reset monthly and then starts from anew.

1. In order to view the amount of minutes used, and the amount still available on 100% discount, search and open the customer profile:




2. At the bottom of the page, select 'Services', and then 'Bundles' to view the current bundle in use by the customer.



3. In order to view the customers available and used minutes, select 'Discounts' under the defined discount plan.

Prepaid Discount Bundle - B

  • Credit limit is set to 0.00. Payment, as defined on the SOF needs to be made monthly before call will be allowed.
  • In this case, there is no discount plan created or added. The per minute rate, equals the amount of minutes. For example: 100 000 minutes' voice bundle @ R19 000 excl. VAT (100 000 / R19 000 = 0.19 Per minute)
  • Billing is done monthly, as per the amount specified on the SOF, payable for the provided voice bundle.
  • Should the available funds be depleted, the customer needs to make another payment to have avialable funds in order to allow outgoing calls.
  • For this discount plan, voice tariffs is pre-defined at a fixed rate, and applicable to SA prefixes ONLY.
  • Discount minutes not used, will be carried over to the next month however, the recurring monthly fee is still expected. This service is not reset monthly.

In order to view funds still available for the customer, search the customer were the available funds will be visible




In this case, there is no discount plan to be reviewed, only the available funds.
