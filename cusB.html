Customer Balance and Credit Limit



Current Balance and Credit Limit

NOTE: On the Customer page, from the available list, the current balance, credit limit and other information is visible, such as 'credit limit exceeded or customer blocked'




1. Once navigated to the selected customer, the current balance and credit limit will also be visible on the right side, under the customers name.




It should be noted that resellers are responsable to set credit limits, upon creating a customer. This serves as an added fraud protection, should any of their customers accounts be hacked. The reseller will be liable for such fees, as the service provider for the customer.

Regarding customer balances and credit limits, further training on balance adjustments will not be done. Credit limit increase or decrease, balance adjustments, customer status such as the 'Block' status, and any payements, refunds, or credits that might be issued should be handled only by the reseller of the customer, unless assistance was rquested.

As an added 'Fraud Protection' measure, there are daily spending plans set, which starts and resets at the beginning of each new day


NOTE: Should the reseller not have the 'Daily Spending Plans' avialable for their clients, and should it be a requirement, please advise VoiceOps in order to assist with creating the requested profiles.


In the case that a customers daily spending limit was reach, the following actions can be taken in order to re-open the account:

  • Reset - This action is recommended in the case that it is a one-time request, and where a daily limit increase is not repeatedly requested.
  • Edit - This action is recommended in the case that the daily limit is increased repeatedly, due to an increase of numbers, or voice usage.

To Reset the Daily Spending Limit:

On the customer page, scroll down to the Fraud protection field.



Under the ‘Fraud protection’ tab, select ‘Spending plan’ and then the ‘CHANGE LIMIT’ option:




Once 'CHANGE LIMIT'was selected, current limit can either be ‘Reset’ to start from anew, or an new limit for the day can be manually typed and updated:




NOTE: This change is applied for the remainder of the current day only.


To Change the Daily Spending Limit:

On the reseller page, scroll down to the Fraud protection field



The current profile can be changed by selecting the 'pencil' icon, or if not already assigned, it can be selected from the dropdown list:



NOTE:By default it will be implemented when the current plan expires, alternatively, you have the option to change with ‘Immediate’ effect.


To change the current daily spending plan, select the new customer daily spending plan from the list.



NOTE: This change is now applied to each following day until changed or removed.



NOTE: Both the Credit Limit and Daily Spending Plan should be setup in a way as to cover calls for all Sip accounts and Aliases assigned to this customer.
