Subcustomers of a Reseller


Viewing Subcustomer List

1. Once navigated to the selected reseller, select 'Subcustomers'.




2. The list of customers will display alphabeticaly, including thier status, balance and credit limits




3. For reseller with alot of customers, the total might not be shown, instead there will be a '+' indicating that there are more customers.




4. By clicking on the blue number with the '+' sign, the total amount of customers for the specific reseller will be shown.
